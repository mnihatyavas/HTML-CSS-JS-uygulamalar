<h2>Sayfa 3: Görünmeyen son?!..</h2>

<p>A kilometer or so more, and the cottage was now in sight — our hero could see lights shining in the windows, and a figure shuffling around within! This was the moment he was waiting for surely! He tiptoed up to the door, and carefully tried the handle. Fnding it unlocked, he burst open the door and shouted "Nihayet yakayı ele verdin, dürzü!..!" Fakat, ağaç katili baltacı yerine hafif korkmuş köpeğiyle yüzyüze gelince hayalkırıklığını gizleyemedi.</p>
